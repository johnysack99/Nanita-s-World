Nanita's Diary

🎬 Nanita the Movie Buff

When she is awake, Nanita is glued to the screen. She has watched more movies and shows than most critics, and she always has strong opinions. Her favourite show is "sex and the city", Her favorite pastime? Quoting dramatic lines with her adorable Polish accent, which makes even the scariest villain sound charming.

🦷 Nanita the Dentist-in-Training

This summer, Nanita signed up for dental training. She takes it very seriously — except when she spends hours debating which scrub style and color makes her look unique. she hates black, chrysolite makes her feel cool, and pink makes her feel unstoppable. she is afraid to buy a scrub and a girl she hates also buys the same one. Decisions, decisions!

Don not let her sleepy nature fool you — Nanita is a beast at COD Mobile. She plays with lightning reflexes, always leading her team to victory. Rumor has it she once won a match while half-asleep, proving she is truly unstoppable.
