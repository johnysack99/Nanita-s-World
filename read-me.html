My Pookie

I hope it is clear by now that someone like me won't spend 4 days creating a website for a doll, no matter how much I love that doll. The objective and scientific representation of such an abstract concept as love is intellectually demanding for a person like me because my mind is scientifically oriented to the extent that objectivity becomes the metaphorical God ruling my mental processes; nevertheless, and for some unexplainable reason beyond my analytical abilities to understand, Objectivity completely collapses when it comes to you, I neither do nor want to understand what this is in its most unmeasurable nature. The universe began as a singularity; perhaps everything we call distance is only the long, dramatic route matter took to bring us together, the latter is a genuine, non-evidence based believe that I have. If this is a simulation, I want us to play it together, not because you are a great player, mostly, and not because I am afraid to be alone, or I am simply following my biological code to find a mate and procreate, but because I do not want to play this game with anyone else but you. No, we don't complete each other; this is what insecure and overly emotional people say. We are already complete as conscious entities aware of our own existence, and that self-sufficient completeness is what makes us inseparable. I don't necessarily believe in the general existence of souls, at least this is a statement that I would have said before knowing you, because you shine too bright, that is a metaphysical property, not physical. No matter what happens to you, never stop shining.
